IntelliStock Privacy Policy

IntelliStock values your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our AI-powered inventory management system. By using IntelliStock, you agree to the practices described in this policy and understand how your information helps improve your store’s operations and app experience.

1. Information We Collect

We collect data such as store information, product inventory levels, sales patterns, and user preferences. This may include automatic system data like timestamps and device information to improve accuracy and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to provide inventory insights, automate restocking notifications, and enhance user experience. Data helps us refine AI predictions and offer better store recommendations.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

Your data is not shared with third parties except as required by law or to maintain system operations such as analytics or security monitoring. All shared data is anonymized and secured.

4. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to ensure the protection of your information. Regular security audits are performed to maintain confidentiality and integrity.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your information. You can also opt out of data collection by adjusting your app settings or contacting our support team.
